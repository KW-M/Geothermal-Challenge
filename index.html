The Future of Geothermal Power

Enough accessible heat lies 7 km under US soils to power 5x the entire nation’s electricity use for millions of years.
(US Department of Energy Geovision Report Estimate)

Until recently, taping into this vast energy source was confined to isolated hotspots...

But change is on the horizon...

New technologies are allowing us to tap geothermal heat almost anywhere in the world and turn it into power for our cities, heat for our homes, and even valuable minerals.
